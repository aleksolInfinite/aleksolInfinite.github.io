About Me

I am a third year ICT student at Metropolia University of Applied Sciences located in Espoo, Finland. My major is game development, mainly focused on programming. I have had a passion for programming and gaming for years now, but I took a few years to think what I really wanted and spent that time chauffeuring. Finally I came to the conclusion that I should start studies in the field of programming. I had my expectations that game programming would be my calling, but went into my studies open minded. My mind hasn't changed, programming and designing games is absolutely what motivates me.

My completed projects are mostly small school projects, but most importantly, for the past 10 months I have acted as a lead programmer in a voluntary group of 5-10 people working on a 3D adventure RPG. We started with Unity, but moved to Unreal Engine 4 last autumn and currently making the transition to UE5. On this website I showcase what I can of the 3D RPG as well as best bits of my school projects.
